Landsat jointed a program of the USGS and NASA and has been observing the Earth continuously from 1972 through the present day. Today the landsat satellites image the entire Eartu's surface at a 30 meter resolution about once every two weeks, including multispectral and thermal data.

For example: Population, Land area, Intensive land use. Nowadays, Cambodia was rapit growth for sector and population.
